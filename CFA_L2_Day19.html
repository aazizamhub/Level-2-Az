Equity Method & Consolidation

Intercorporate Investments — Subtopic 1 of 2

60 min total
30 min theory
30 min practice
~12% exam weight
Learning Objectives
By the end of this subtopic, you'll be able to:
  • Bedakan tiga kategori investasi (financial assets <20%, associates 20-50%, business combinations >50%) dan metode akuntansinya
  • Hitung carrying value investasi menggunakan equity method — termasuk amortisasi excess purchase price dan goodwill
  • Jelaskan acquisition method untuk business combinations dan dampaknya terhadap financial statements (full consolidation)
  • Terapkan eliminasi unrealized profit pada upstream dan downstream transactions antara investor dan associate
Key Concepts
1
Klasifikasi Intercorporate Investments

Intercorporate investments diklasifikasikan berdasarkan level of influence yang dimiliki investor atas investee. Ini menentukan metode akuntansi yang dipakai.

Ownership % Level of Influence Accounting Method
<20% No significant influence Fair value (IFRS 9): FVPL, FVOCI, or amortized cost
20-50% Significant influence Equity method ("one-line consolidation")
>50% Control Acquisition method (full consolidation)

IFRS 9 Financial Assets (<20%): Tiga kategori measurement — amortized cost, FVPL (fair value through profit or loss), FVOCI (fair value through OCI). Klasifikasi berdasarkan business model test + cash flow characteristics test.

Key point: Batas 20% dan 50% itu PRESUMPTION, bukan absolute rule. Perusahaan bisa punya significant influence dengan <20% (e.g., board representation, material transactions) atau nggak punya significant influence meskipun >20%.
Exam Trap
IFRS 9: pilihan FVOCI untuk equity instruments itu IRREVOCABLE. Sekali milih, nggak bisa ganti. Dan untuk equity instruments di FVOCI, hanya dividend income yang masuk P&L — unrealized gains/losses tetap di OCI dan TIDAK di-recycled ke P&L.
2
Equity Method: "One-Line Consolidation"

Equity method dipakai saat investor punya significant influence (biasanya 20-50%). Disebut "one-line consolidation" karena:

  • Balance sheet: Satu baris — "Investment in Associate"
  • Income statement: Satu baris — "Equity in income of associate"

Mekanisme equity method:

Investment balance = Cost + Investor's share of investee's income − Dividends received − Amortization of excess purchase price

Dividends MENGURANGI carrying value (return of capital), BUKAN income!

Contoh dari kurikulum: Parker beli 30% Prince seharga €500,000. Prince book value net assets = €1,200,000. Fair value net assets = €1,500,000. Plant & equipment undervalued €300,000 (10-year life).

Excess purchase price = €500,000 − (30% × €1,200,000) = €140,000 → Attributable to PP&E: 30% × €300,000 = €90,000 → Goodwill (residual): €140,000 − €90,000 = €50,000 Annual amortization of PP&E excess: €90,000 ÷ 10 = €9,000/year

Setelah tahun 1 (Prince income €100K, dividends €50K):

Investment balance = €500,000 + (30% × €100,000) − (30% × €50,000) − €9,000 = €506,000
Key: Goodwill TIDAK diamortisasi — indefinite life. Tapi goodwill di equity method TIDAK ditest impairment secara terpisah; yang di-test adalah entire carrying amount of investment.
3
Acquisition Method (Full Consolidation)

Kalau investor punya control (>50%), wajib pakai acquisition method. Semua assets dan liabilities investee di-consolidate 100% ke parent's financial statements — meskipun parent cuma punya 60%.

Tiga jenis business combination:

  • Merger: A + B = A (B ceases to exist)
  • Acquisition: A + B = (A + B) sebagai parent-subsidiary, keduanya tetap exist
  • Consolidation: A + B = C (entitas baru, A dan B cease)

Steps in acquisition method:

  • 1. Identify the acquirer
  • 2. Determine acquisition date
  • 3. Measure consideration transferred (at fair value)
  • 4. Recognize and measure identifiable assets/liabilities at FAIR VALUE
  • 5. Recognize goodwill = Purchase price − Fair value of net identifiable assets
Exam Trap
Perbedaan equity method vs acquisition: Equity method hanya satu baris di BS dan IS. Acquisition method → 100% assets/liabilities masuk consolidated BS (even jika cuma own 60%). Ini bikin total assets JAUH lebih besar, debt ratios lebih tinggi, tapi net income SAMA karena ada non-controlling interest deduction.
4
Unrealized Profit: Upstream vs Downstream

Kalau ada transaksi antara investor dan associate, profit yang belum "confirmed" (barang belum dijual ke pihak ketiga) harus di-eliminate dari equity income.

Upstream sale = associate jual ke investor. Profit tercatat di IS associate → masuk equity income investor via share of income.

Downstream sale = investor jual ke associate. Profit tercatat di IS investor langsung.

Keduanya: investor harus eliminate share of unrealized profit.

Equity income adjustment = Investor's % × Unrealized profit in ending inventory

Contoh upstream: Foxworth (associate) jual inventory ke Wicker (25% owner). Ada €8,000 unrealized profit di inventory Wicker. Adjustment = 25% × €8,000 = €2,000 dikurangi dari equity income.

Contoh downstream: Jones (investor, 25%) jual ke Jason (associate). Cost €96K dijual €160K (margin 40%). Jason masih pegang €40K di inventory. Unrealized profit = 40% × €40K = €16K. Jones's share = 25% × €16K = €4,000.

Timing: Unrealized profit di-defer di period penjualan, lalu di-reverse (realized) di period berikutnya saat barang dijual ke third party. Zero net effect over time.
Quick Reference — Cheat Sheet
Equity Method Investment Balance
Cost + Share of income − Dividends − Amortization of excess purchase price
Dividends = return of capital, NOT income
Goodwill (Equity Method)
Goodwill = Purchase price − Investor's share of FV of net identifiable assets
NOT amortized, NOT separately tested for impairment
IFRS 9 Classifications
Debt: Amortized cost, FVOCI, FVPL
Equity: FVPL or FVOCI (irrevocable)
Business model + cash flow test
Acquisition Method
100% of assets/liabilities consolidated at FAIR VALUE. Non-controlling interest shown separately.

Practice Questions — Indonesian Vignette (30 min)
Vignette
Dewi Rahayu adalah senior analyst di PT Mandala Investama, sebuah asset management firm di Jakarta. Dia sedang menganalisis investasi baru yang dilakukan oleh PT Nusantara Jaya (NJ), perusahaan konglomerat yang listed di IDX.

Investasi 1: NJ membeli 25% saham PT Borneo Energi (BE) seharga Rp 400 milyar. Book value net assets BE = Rp 1.200 milyar, fair value net assets = Rp 1.400 milyar. Selisih fair value vs book value sepenuhnya berasal dari PP&E yang undervalued (sisa umur 8 tahun, straight-line). Di tahun pertama, BE melaporkan net income Rp 160 milyar dan membayar dividen Rp 40 milyar. Selama tahun berjalan, BE menjual inventory ke NJ senilai Rp 20 milyar dengan profit Rp 8 milyar. Inventory tersebut BELUM dijual NJ ke pihak ketiga per akhir tahun.

Investasi 2: NJ juga membeli 5% saham PT Sulawesi Mineral (SM) seharga Rp 50 milyar. NJ mengklasifikasikan investasi ini sebagai FVOCI di bawah IFRS 9. Di akhir tahun, fair value investasi SM naik menjadi Rp 62 milyar. SM membayar dividen Rp 2 milyar ke NJ.
Question 1
Berapa goodwill yang termasuk dalam purchase price investasi NJ di BE?
A) Rp 100 milyar
B) Rp 50 milyar
C) Rp 150 milyar
Explanation: Excess purchase price = Rp 400M − (25% × Rp 1,200M) = Rp 400M − Rp 300M = Rp 100M. Attributable to PP&E = 25% × (Rp 1,400M − Rp 1,200M) = 25% × Rp 200M = Rp 50M. Goodwill (residual) = Rp 100M − Rp 50M = Rp 50 milyar.
Question 2
Berapa equity income yang dilaporkan NJ dari investasi BE untuk tahun pertama?
A) Rp 31.75 milyar
B) Rp 40 milyar
C) Rp 33.75 milyar
Explanation: Share of income = 25% × Rp 160M = Rp 40M. Amortization of excess PP&E = Rp 50M ÷ 8 = Rp 6.25M. Unrealized profit (upstream) = 25% × Rp 8M = Rp 2M. Equity income = Rp 40M − Rp 6.25M − Rp 2M = Rp 31.75 milyar. Dividends do NOT affect equity income — they reduce carrying value.
Question 3
Untuk investasi SM (FVOCI equity instrument), mana yang benar mengenai pelaporan tahun pertama?
A) Unrealized gain Rp 12M diakui di P&L, dividen Rp 2M diakui di P&L
B) Unrealized gain Rp 12M diakui di OCI, dividen Rp 2M diakui di OCI
C) Unrealized gain Rp 12M diakui di OCI, dividen Rp 2M diakui di P&L
Explanation: Untuk equity instruments classified FVOCI under IFRS 9: unrealized gains/losses go to OCI (Rp 62M − Rp 50M = Rp 12M → OCI). Hanya dividend income yang diakui di profit or loss (Rp 2M → P&L). Ini berbeda dari debt FVOCI, di mana gains/losses eventually get recycled to P&L. Equity FVOCI gains/losses NEVER get recycled.
Question 4
Berapa carrying value investasi NJ di BE pada akhir tahun pertama?
A) Rp 431.75 milyar
B) Rp 421.75 milyar
C) Rp 400 milyar
Explanation: Carrying value = Cost + Equity income − Dividends received. = Rp 400M + Rp 31.75M − (25% × Rp 40M) = Rp 400M + Rp 31.75M − Rp 10M = Rp 421.75 milyar. Remember: equity income already includes the amortization and unrealized profit adjustments. Dividends REDUCE carrying value (return of capital).
